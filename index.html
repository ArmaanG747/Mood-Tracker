Mood Tracker

The Mood Tracker is a simple and intuitive web tool that allows you to log your daily mood using emojis and track your emotional trends over time.

With features like mood history, timeline view, and a planned calendar display, this tracker helps you become more aware of your emotional patterns.

How to Use Mood Tracker:

  • Pick Your Mood – Choose an emoji that matches your current emotion.
  • Log Your Mood – Click the "Log Mood" button to save your entry.
  • View Past Moods – See your past moods displayed in a timeline format.
  • Calendar View – Get a visual representation of your emotions over time.
  • Filter by Time – (Coming Soon) View moods for a specific day, week, or month.
